Learn About Us

Best School For Your Kids

AIS-L is committed to providing a American standard of education. The curriculum is that of an U.S. academic preparatory and high school. The school attempts to provide the most up-to-date American methods of teaching and to maintain small classes where maximum interaction among pupils and between teachers and pupils creates an enthusiastic and friendly climate for academic achievement. Students are encouraged to use multiple resources and techniques in order to master skills and acquire new knowledge. Although memorization is sometimes necessary and useful, in most cases we help children to learn by inquiry, reflection, experiment, and discovery. Homework is given at all levels (except Nursery and Pre-K), but in amounts appropriate to grade. We try to be attentive to the special needs of each pupil and make a sincere effort to provide remediation and enrichment when needed and to the best of our ability.
